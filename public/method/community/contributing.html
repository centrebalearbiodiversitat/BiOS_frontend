    Contributing

    Contributions from the community are most welcomed and help Balearica continue growing and innovating!

    Balearica is a dynamic and constantly evolving platform that caters to a wide range of users with diverse needs and backgrounds. To ensure the demands of our user-base are met, the dedicated team of experts behind the platform focus on efficiently evaluating change requests, addressing bug reports, and implementing improvements to maintain and enhance the platform.

    How you can contribute

    Become a Taxon Expert

    If you want to actively contribute to Balearica, you can become a Taxon Expert!

    How It Works

    As a Taxon Expert, your role will involve:

    • Reviewing taxa of interest already present in our database (taxonomy, distribution, etc.).
    • Adding missing but essential information required for our system (i.e. habitat, directives, etc.).
    • Sharing your data (e.g., species occurrences) with the scientific community. If you have valuable biodiversity data, we can assist in integrating it into our platform after a review process.

    All personal contributions will be acknowledged in the corresponding entries within Balearica. Given the constant and ongoing scientific debate surrounding taxonomy, distribution, and the autecology of taxa, we aim to address the most relevant issues and carefully consider all contributions provided.

    How to Get Started

    Simply contact us via email, specifying your taxon group of interest and providing a brief overview of your background. Our team will review your profile, and if it aligns with our project’s needs, we will reach out to discuss a potential collaboration.

    Your expertise will help enhance biodiversity knowledge and contribute to a growing scientific resource!

    More details are provided in the specific section Taxon Expert.

    Report a bug

    Reporting bugs, submitting change requests, participating in discussions, and proposing new features can be time-consuming, and at times, it may feel like a thankless or unproductive task. However, these contributions are crucial for Balearica’s development team, as they play a key role in improving the quality of our product and ensuring we provide better service to our users with every update.

    To streamline this process, we plan to establish clear guidelines for dynamic interaction with Balearica. In line with this goal, we will introduce user-friendly features that allow the community to submit issues and participate in discussions more easily, thereby enhancing both the efficiency and overall quality of the platform.

    To date, if you would like to report an issue or request a change, you can email our team with the details of your request. Please include in the mail:

    • A clear description of the issue or request.
    • Steps to reproduce (if applicable).
    • Any relevant screenshots or examples.

    We appreciate your contributions in helping us improve Balearica!

    Stay tuned to our Blog for updates on new features that will further streamline this process.

    Improve Documentation

    Writing software documentation is a challenging task that requires time and effort to structure content in a clear, organized, and visually appealing way. We are committed to creating comprehensive and user-friendly documentation. However, if you wish to improve the text quality, suggest better structures, or contribute methodologies not yet covered, your input is most welcome. Every suggestion will be carefully considered, and if it adds value to the Balearica documentation, it will be integrated.
