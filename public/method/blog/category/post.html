    Balearica: Update to version 1.1.0

    Hi folks! 👋

    We released the new version of Balearica [v1.1.0], packed with new data, improved visuals, and smarter tools for your research!

    Check out our full list of changes in the CHANGELOG. Here’s a quick overview of what’s new!

    More Taxa, More Data

    Our dataset just got bigger! We’ve expanded Balearica with new taxa, occurrences, and sequences.

    We also added:

    • Taxa: from 13,783 to 16,610 (+2,827)

    • Occurrences: from 1,397,426 to 1,629,797 (+232,371)

    • Sequences: from 350,646 to 498,310 (+147,664)

    This means broader coverage and more comprehensive data for Balearic biodiversity research!

    New Additions

    Balearica development keeps going strong!

    Highlights include:

    • Initial release of the documentation, offering a clearer and more structured guide to the platform.
    • New Source Ranking page and Source Profiles for a better understanding of data provenance.
    • Genetic Marker synonyms, making it easier to search genetic data.
    • GBIF dataset key integration for improved data linking and interoperability.
    • AI-assisted taxonomy retrieval, adding new records through intelligent data enhancement.

    Changes & Improvements

    This version also comes with several design and performance improvements to make your experience smoother and more cohesive:

    • Redesigned Home Page, featuring an updated visual identity and layout.
    • Updated logo and branding, reflecting Balearica’s growing vision.
    • Restyled Source Page for a consistent and modern appearance.
    • Improved loading times thanks to a brand-new caching system.

    Fixes

    We’ve taken care of a few issues to ensure greater accuracy and reliability:

    • Updated to IUCN v4 classification.
    • Fixed minor map display issues for occurrence points.

    Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for being part of the Balearica community!

    – The Balearica Team –

    Balearica: A New Platform for Balearic Biodiversity Research

    We are thrilled to announce the official launch of Balearica [v1.0.0], a groundbreaking biodiversity platform developed by a group of experts at the Centre Balear de Biodiversitat. This state-of-the-art public platform is designed to collect, manage, analyze, and share invaluable biodiversity data from the Balearic Islands—all in one accessible hub.

    A Comprehensive View of Balearic Biodiversity

    Balearica integrates diverse data sources, including field observations, museum records, genetic data, and more, creating an unparalleled resource for understanding the region’s ecosystems. By combining these data streams, the platform provides useful insights for scientific research and conservation efforts.

    Who Is Balearica For?

    Whether you’re a scientist, researcher, policymaker, conservationist, or a nature enthusiast, Balearica is built for you. Designed to be both intuitive and accessible, it serves as a powerful tool for exploring the Balearic Islands’ rich biodiversity, supporting in-depth research, and enabling data-driven conservation decisions.

    Empowering Conservation Through Innovation

    Balearica has the potential to become an indispensable resource for protecting the Balearic Islands’ unique ecosystems. With its user-friendly interface and integrated data, it empowers researchers and policymakers to take informed, impactful actions that drive conservation forward.

    Join Us in Protecting Balearic Biodiversity

    As we continue to expand and enhance Balearica’s capabilities, we invite you to be part of this journey. Your feedback and contributions will be invaluable in shaping the future of this platform.

    Stay tuned for more updates, and thank you for being part of the Balearica community!

    – The Balearica Team –
